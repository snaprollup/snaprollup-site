What SnapRollup does

SnapRollup is an add-on for Google Sheets™ that reads the data in your active sheet, groups it by a column of your choice, and computes the metrics you need. The result is written to a new sheet named "Resumen" within the same file.

Simple grouping

Pick any column to group by (customers, products, dates, categories) and SnapRollup builds the summary automatically.

Six operations

Sum, average, count, maximum, minimum, and mode. Apply them to multiple columns in a single pass.

Fast and lightweight

Processes thousands of rows in memory without blocking the spreadsheet. Single-pass read and write.

Your data stays in place

All processing runs inside your own Google account. No data is transmitted to external servers.

Works across all your sheets

Once installed, SnapRollup is available from the side panel in every spreadsheet you open.

Minimal permissions

Access is limited to the file you open the add-on in. Other files in your drive are not accessed.

How to use it

  1. Open any spreadsheet containing tabular data.
  2. Open SnapRollup from the right-hand side panel.
  3. Click "Load columns".
  4. Choose the column you want to group by.
  5. Add one or more metrics (column + operation).
  6. Click "Generate Summary".

Done. A sheet named "Resumen" is created (or overwritten) with the results.

Permissions requested

SnapRollup requests only the permissions strictly required for its functionality:

  • Access to the active file only: limited to the spreadsheet in which the add-on is opened, not to other files in your drive.
  • Read and write access within that file: needed to read your data and write the "Resumen" output sheet.
