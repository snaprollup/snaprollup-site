Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 8, 2026

This Privacy Policy describes how SnapRollup ("the application", "the add-on") handles information when you use it within Google Sheets™. SnapRollup is designed around a simple principle: process your data without transmitting it to any external server.

1. Information SnapRollup Collects

SnapRollup does not collect, store, or transmit personal information to external servers. All add-on logic runs within the Google Apps Script™ environment, under your own Google account.

When you use SnapRollup, the code accesses:

  • The contents of the spreadsheet you have currently open in Google Sheets™ at the moment of using the add-on.
  • Your selection of columns and operations, which is temporarily stored in your user session cache to preserve the form between interactions.

2. How Information Is Used

The data from your spreadsheet is used exclusively for the following purposes:

  • Reading the data from the active sheet you choose to process.
  • Computing the metrics (sum, average, count, maximum, minimum, mode) you requested.
  • Writing the results into a sheet named "SnapRollup" within the same spreadsheet file.

Your data is never transmitted to external servers, third parties, or the developer. Processing occurs entirely within the Google infrastructure.

3. Permissions Requested (OAuth Scopes)

SnapRollup requests the following minimum permissions:

  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/spreadsheets.currentonly — Grants read and write access only to the spreadsheet currently open. The add-on cannot access other spreadsheets in your drive.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/drive.file — Required by Google Workspace™ Add-ons that interact with files. Limited to file-scope, meaning only the file in which the add-on is opened.
  • https://www.googleapis.com/auth/script.locale — Used to respect the user's locale and time zone when formatting dates in the output.

4. Temporary Storage (Cache)

To preserve form configuration as you navigate within the add-on (for example, when adding or removing metrics), SnapRollup temporarily stores user-interface state in the CacheService of Google Apps Script™, scoped exclusively to your user session.

This cache:

  • Expires automatically after 30 minutes of inactivity.
  • Is accessible only by your own session.
  • Does not contain the data of your spreadsheet, only UI metadata (names of selected columns, chosen operation, etc.).

5. Cookies and Tracking

SnapRollup does not use cookies, does not include any tracking scripts, and does not rely on any third-party analytics services.

6. Sharing with Third Parties

SnapRollup does not share any information with third parties. There are no external servers, no third-party APIs invoked by the add-on, and no integrations with external services.

7. Compliance with Google Policies

SnapRollup complies with the Google API Services User Data Policy, including the Limited Use requirements.

8. Data Deletion

Because SnapRollup does not store any data on any server, there is nothing to delete on the developer's side. To revoke the add-on's access to your Google account at any time, visit https://myaccount.google.com/permissions.

9. Changes to This Policy

If this policy is updated in the future, the revised version will be published on this same page, along with its "Last updated" date. Significant changes will also be communicated on the home page.

10. Contact

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please reach out through the Support page.
