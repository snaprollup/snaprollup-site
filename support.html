Frequently Asked Questions

Does SnapRollup send my data to any external server?

No. All processing takes place within the Google Apps Script™ environment under your own account. Your data does not leave the Google ecosystem. See the Privacy Policy for more details.

Does it work with any spreadsheet?

Yes, as long as it contains tabular data (rows as records, columns as attributes). If your sheet has headers in the first row, make sure to leave the corresponding switch enabled.

What happens if a sheet named "Resumen" already exists?

SnapRollup overwrites it. If you have important content in a sheet with that name, please make a copy before generating the summary.

How many rows can it process?

SnapRollup is designed to handle datasets of up to several tens of thousands of rows. If your sheet is very large, processing may take longer due to the execution-time limits imposed by Google Apps Script™.

What should I do if I see an error?

Please write to the contact email above, including:

  • A description of the issue.
  • The error message that appeared, if any.
  • The approximate number of rows and columns in the sheet.

How do I uninstall SnapRollup?

From Google Sheets™, go to Extensions → Add-ons → Manage add-ons, locate SnapRollup, and click "Uninstall". You may also revoke access at myaccount.google.com/permissions.
